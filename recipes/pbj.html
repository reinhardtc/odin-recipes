Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

A peanut butter and jelly sandwich is a classic, no-cook combination of creamy or crunchy peanut butter and your favorite fruit preserves layered between two slices of soft bread. Simple to assemble in minutes, it delivers a satisfying balance of rich, nutty flavor and sweet fruitiness in every bite.

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Lay two slices of bread flat on a clean surface.
  2. Spread a generous layer of peanut butter evenly on one slice of bread.
  3. Spread your desired amount of jelly or jam evenly on the other slice of bread.
  4. Press the two slices together, peanut butter and jelly sides facing each other.
  5. Cut diagonally if desired, and serve immediately.
